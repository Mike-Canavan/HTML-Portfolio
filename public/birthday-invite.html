It's My Birthday!

When will it be?:

At 2:00 pm on my birthday.

What should you bring?:

  • Your love for and admiration of me.
  • A present. I like getting presents
  • Food. I will be hungry.

Where will it be?:

Six Flags St Louis

We will gather 'round me at the main entrance.

What will we do?:

  • Starting at the main entrance we will sing Happy Birthday in joyous adoration.
  • You will then follow me into the park and we will go to a ride of my choosing.
